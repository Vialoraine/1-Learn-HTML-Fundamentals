Intoduction to HTML: Displaying Text

If you want to display text in HTML, you can use both a paragraph (p tag) or a span tag.

    Paragraphs or p tags: contain a block of plain text
    Span tags or span: contain short pieces of text or other HTML. They are used to seperate small pieces of content that are on the same line as other content.

Its best to use a span element when you want to target a specific piece of content that is inline, or on the same line as other text. If you want to divide your content into blocks, its better to use a div.
